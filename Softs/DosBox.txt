====== DosBox ======
For playing Dos games under Linux
https://www.dosbox.com/DOSBoxManual.html

**Installation**
''apt install dosbox''

**Full screen**
''Atl+Enter''

**Change keyboard mapping**
''keyb fr''

**Mount a local drive**
''mount c'' [[~/mygame|''~/mygame'']]
then ''c:''
