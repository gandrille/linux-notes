==== Non fully open source software ====
**Collabora**
* [[https://www.collaboraoffice.com/code/|CODE (Collabora Online Development Edition)]]
	* free for test/home users, limited to 10 open documents only
	* [[https://hub.docker.com/r/collabora/code/|docker image]]
	* Integration with OwnCloud: [[https://www.collaboraoffice.com/code/quick-tryout-owncloud-docker/|for quick tests]] - [[https://www.collaboraoffice.com/code/docker-2/|for production environments]]
* [[https://www.collaboraoffice.com/collabora-online/|Collabora Online (Enterprise version)]]: paid
* [[https://www.collaboraoffice.com/solutions/collabora-office/|Collabora Office]]: paid. Collabora Online with advanced admin UI,...

**ONLYOFFICE**
* [[https://www.onlyoffice.com/fr/|ONLYOFFICE]] only provides paid plans.
