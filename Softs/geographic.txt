====== geographic ======

===== Marble (KDE) =====
A a very good [[https://www.osgeo.org/|OSGeo]] project.
* package: ''marble''
* [[https://marble.kde.org/|Website]]

===== viking =====
Viking is a free/open source program to manage GPS data. You can import, export and plot tracks, routes and waypoints, show various OpenStreetMap, Bing Aerial and other maps in it.
* package: ''viking''
* [[https://sourceforge.net/projects/viking/|Website]]

===== QMapShack =====
QMapShack is the next generation of the famous QLandkarte GT application.
* package: ''qmapshack''
* [[https://github.com/Maproom/qmapshack|GitHub]]
* [[https://github.com/Maproom/qmapshack/wiki/DocMain|Manual]]
