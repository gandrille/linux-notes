====== Autostart Specification ======
[[https://specifications.freedesktop.org/autostart-spec/autostart-spec-latest.html|Desktop Application Autostart Specification]]

By default, ''~/.config/autostart/'' and ''/etc/xdg/autostart/'' can hold .desktop files that will be lauched at session startup.
